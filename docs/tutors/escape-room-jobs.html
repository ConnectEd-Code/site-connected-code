Incursion Events — Paid Tutoring Job (2–3 days)

Location: Presbyterian Ladies' College Sydney

ConnectEd Code is delivering a huge event this year for PLC Sydney (in Croydon). I'm looking for ~20 staff to help at this event. We'll be helping the children build escape rooms with tech, so it'll be a lot of fun!

This event is coming up soon (8 weeks away) so we're very keen to hear from anyone who wants to be involved.

How to Sign Up!

Tutors will be paid between $30 and $45 an hour depending on the role and your experience level.

If you're interested please fill out this form!

You can also email renee@connectedcode.org to ask questions.

Are You Interested?

If you're interested you should come along to the info and learning session. (Date and Time TBC, week of the 22nd of August)

The purpose of the session is:

  • To understand what experience you already have in coding, embedded systems, and teaching
  • Give you a chance to learn Kookaberry skills and see if you would be comfortable teaching those
  • See how you'd work as part of the team

Preferable skills:

  • Experience coding Kookaberries, Micro:Bits, or other MicroPython embedded systems
  • Confidence in Python and debugging (variables, loops, if statements)
  • Experience teaching Python to high school students

Event Info — Escape Room Building (Year 8) — September 19th, 20th, 22nd

A three day event during school hours (Monday, Tuesday, Thursday) where we'll be teaching all of Year 8 (160 girls) coding in a fun "build an escape room" theme.

On day one students will have classes on how to use various components, like radio, lights, buttons, etc.

On days 2 and 3 students will work in teams to create a small scale escape room prototype using the knowledge they gained on day 1, with ciphers, keys, locks and hidden messages.

Over the days you will be helping deliver classes on day 1 and then working with groups to help them create their contraptions on days 2 and 3, helping them debug, and keeping them on track.

Connecting coding with context, community & curriculum.
