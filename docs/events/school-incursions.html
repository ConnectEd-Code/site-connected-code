Engaging Incursions at Your School

Data DeTechtives

Students will build and program devices such as moisture sensors and altimeters, as well as cracking codes, to solve a murder mystery.

Escape Artists

Groups will collaborate to design and build their very own escape room, filled with electronics, then solve each other's rooms.

These incursions are mapped to the Stage 4 curriculum and are designed as 2 or 3 day programs for high school students, though they can be modified if you're seeking an event for a different cohort. They're a great option if you want to fit as much of the curriculum in as short a time as possible, while giving your teachers time for other work such as marking.

We bring all the tutors, workbooks, and equipment. All the students need is access to a computer.

Want to enquire about an incursion?

Let us know how we can help you by telling us about the kind of event you are looking for. Tell us how many students and what grades they are in, how long a session you want and anything else you want us to know.

We look forward to creating a custom incursion experience for you!
