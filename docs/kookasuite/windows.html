Windows — How to Install KookaSuite

Step 1. Click the Download button below:

Download for Windows ⬇️
Step 2. Run the downloaded file called "KookaSuite-1_7-Win64.exe" by clicking on it.
Step 3. If your computer shows a blue box, click "More info". We promise the software is safe — it's just new and not officially released yet! (If your computer doesn't show this box, skip to Step 5.)
Step 4. Click "Run anyway".
Step 5. Click Next to start the setup.
Step 6. Click Next to continue.
Step 7. Click Next to install all the modules.
Step 8. Click Next to continue.
Step 9. Click Install and wait for the installation.

You did it! Great work!

Now you can close this window and open KookaIDE from your Start menu!
