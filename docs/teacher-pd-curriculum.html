2024 Curriculum Changes

With the changes to the NSW Software Design and Development curriculum, we offer PD workshops that can help bring you up to speed on the changes.

We'll go over the changes to the curriculum, what it all means, and how to teach it. We'll also offer suggestions of projects that you could teach, mapped directly to the curriculum points.

Interested in teacher PD sessions for your school?

Let us know! Tell us where you are, how many teachers we'd be working with, and how many sessions you would like.

We look forward to helping you out!
